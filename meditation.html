Meditation Techniques

Explore different ways to practice mindfulness and find what works for you.

Mindfulness (Vipassana)

The practice of non-judgmental awareness. Observe your thoughts and sensations as they arise and pass away, returning gently to your breath each time your mind wanders.

Loving-Kindness (Metta)

Focus on developing feelings of goodwill, kindness, and warmth towards yourself and others by silently repeating phrases like "May I be happy, may I be well."

Body Scan

Mentally scan your body from head to toe, noticing any areas of tension or sensation. This practice builds deep somatic awareness and promotes physical relaxation.

Zen (Zazen)

Simple seated meditation focused on posture and breath. It emphasizes "just sitting" (shikantaza) without seeking any specific state of mind.

Box Breathing Exercise

Follow the circle: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4.
